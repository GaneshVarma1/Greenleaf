Events at GreenLeaf Stay

At GreenLeaf Stay, we offer a variety of enriching and exciting events designed to enhance your stay and provide you with unforgettable experiences. Nestled in the heart of Kerala's picturesque tea plantations, our events blend adventure, culture, and relaxation, ensuring there's something for everyone.

Daily at 9:00 AM and 3:00 PM

Elephant Ride to Waterfalls

Experience the thrill of an elephant ride through the lush tea plantations, leading you to a stunning waterfall hidden deep within the hills. This unique adventure allows you to connect with nature in a truly special way. As you ride, enjoy the gentle sway and the breathtaking views of the verdant landscape. Once you reach the waterfall, take some time to relax and soak in the beauty of this natural wonder. This unforgettable journey combines excitement and serenity. Please book at least 24 hours in advance to secure your spot.

Time: Daily at 7:00 PM

Traditional Kerala Dance Performances

Immerse yourself in the rich cultural heritage of Kerala with our traditional dance performances. Witness the graceful and expressive movements of Kathakali, a classical Indian dance-drama known for its elaborate costumes and vibrant makeup. Our performances also feature Mohiniyattam, the enchanting dance of the enchantress, characterized by its graceful movements and rhythmic footwork. These cultural evenings provide a captivating insight into the artistic traditions of Kerala, making your stay even more memorable. Advance booking 24 hours prior is recommended.

Time: Daily at 10:00 AM and 2:00 PM

Houseboat Ride

Embark on a serene houseboat ride along the tranquil backwaters of Kerala. Our traditional Kettuvallam (houseboat) offers a comfortable and picturesque journey through the interconnected lagoons, lakes, and rivers. Enjoy the lush green scenery, spot local wildlife, and experience the unique lifestyle of the people living along the backwaters. A houseboat ride is a quintessential Kerala experience, offering peace, relaxation, and a close connection with nature. Please book at least 24 hours in advance to ensure availability.
