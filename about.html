Welcome to GreenLeaf!

Welcome to GreenLeaf Stay, a serene and picturesque retreat nestled in the heart of Kerala's lush tea plantations. Our tranquil haven offers an idyllic escape from the hustle and bustle of everyday life, providing you with an immersive experience of nature's beauty and the rich culture of Kerala. At GreenLeaf Stay, we promise a rejuvenating experience surrounded by verdant hills, fragrant tea gardens, and unparalleled hospitality.
